## 2 Asymptotic Analysis



### 2.1 “Big-Oh” notation



> $T(n)$ is said to be $O(f(n))$ iff there exists $c, n_0>0$, s.t. 
> $$
> \forall n>n_0, ~T(n) \leqslant c\cdot f(n)
> $$
